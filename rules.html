READ BEFORE Joining!

Members must be "expats" based in WARSAW POLAND Mission Statement: EIWP exists to create a positive and constructive environment to meet and help fellow expats in Warsaw, Poland. We welcome humble self-promotion and networking between expats. GROUP RULES:
  1. No companies can join! This is a group for real PEOPLE who are expats in Warsaw, Poland. Spammy or suspicious posters removed at the complete discretion of the admin.
  2. Post a complete idea! All posts must clearly state who/what/when/why in the TEXT of the ORIGINAL POST. Events or posts where a reader needs to PM for basic details will be removed.
  3. HUMBLE self-promotion - large/intrusive ads will be removed. Repeat offenders are banned. Company profiles or excessive posting of products will be removed. Users banned at the discretion of the admin. If you are a company, email FIRST.
  4. SELLING or Promoting- The ORIGINAL POST (not comments) must contain the price or price range of the item in question. Ads should be 100% clear about what is offered. Pictures and text should not take excessive space. Sell things you personally own. Commercial Advertisers or brokers will be removed. If you represent an organization, SEE RULE #10.
  5. Job OFFERS must have a CLEAR description of the job, salary or salary range. It must be clear WHO is offering the job from either the original post. Otherwise, it will be removed and the poster banned.
  6. No SPAM! Anything the admin or enough users complain about as being SPAMMY will be removed. If in doubt, the admin has the last word.
  7. YOU agree to help by reporting those who do not abide by these rules. YOU keep this community clean by telling others to keep the rules. Your comments help find the trash and keep it out.
  8. You agree the admin is a benevolent dictator of this group and as such may choose to remove, ban, poke fun at, or be the giver of biting satire at any point and without any reason. ;-)
  9. English language group! Primary language must be English, otherwise, post may be deleted.
  10. Advertisers/Commercial sellers/agents - Email us about posting ONLY if you are willing to buy a round of drinks and appetizers at the next EIWP event. If you do without asking, you agree to pay 400 zł minimum charge + VAT per post.
  11. Charities - Charities directly related to expats in Warsaw, Poland are allowed to post if the post is in English. Any charity not directly related to Warsaw, Poland, AND the expat community will be removed, poster banned.
If you do not agree 100% to ALL of this, leave the group immediately! Remember, the views/opinions/comments/posts shared in this group are that of the original poster and not necessarily of anyone else.
