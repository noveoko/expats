Community Guidelines

This community is open to anyone but expats & repatriates living in Warsaw, Poland are especially encouraged to join.

Membership qualification

Members must meet at least one of these requirements

  • Citzenship in a country that is not Poland
  • Citzenship in a country that is not Poland & Poland (dual citizens)
  • Permanent residence status in a country that is not Poland but currently residing in Warsaw, Poland (and it's environs)
  • University degree from abroad but currently reside in Warsaw, Poland (and it's environs)
  • Employment by a foreign government (embassy, consulate etc.)in Warsaw, Poland.
  • Fluent in English and resident in Warsaw, Poland

Mission Statement

This community exists to develop a positive and constructive environment to meet and help fellow expats in Warsaw, Poland. We welcome humble self-promotion and networking between expats. The higher quality and relevance your post has the better chances of it getting accepted.

Community Membership Rules

People not Organizations

Participation in this community is strictly limited to private individuals. If you are the employee/owner/shareholder of a company that wants to promote to our community please reach out directly to request a commerical membership.

High-quality content

Posts must include a detailed body that expresses the complete idea behind a given post. Posts that rely on links to third-party sites will receive a lower approval score, and may be denied altogether.

Self-promotion without going overboard

Feel free to promote your event or product but only if it specifically applies to our community and you are the owner/developer of the proudct. Content that "could apply to anyone on Earth" is discouraged for it's generality.

Job Offers

Note: All suspicious/low quality job ads will be removed.

Warning! This community does not take responsibility for the content of job posts. Apply at your own risk!

Job offers are welcome as long as they include:

  • A clear description of the job responsibilities
  • Salary or salary range (within limits set by the Polish government regarding min. wages).
  • Full legal company name with address in Poland. For foreign-based companies looking for promote to our community please consider a commerical membership.
  • Expected duration of employment (1 month, 6 months. etc.)
  • Contract type

No Junk/Garbage/Drivel

All posts must:

  • Not be generated by a bot
  • Must not be so general as to be applicable to anyone on Earth (example: join my Excel webinar).
  • Include details and calls to action
  • Contain high-quality content that doesn't seek to offend, promote hate, or violate any human rights or privacy rights.
  • Must be written with a professional quality
  • Must not be a rant

Self policing

Please flag suspicious posts!

The buck stops with the admins

Admins of this community reserve the right to remove any post or comment for any reason in order to maintain a civil and just community.

All content must be in English

Posts written in a foreign-language are not permitted unless they also include an English translation in the text.

Commerical Posters/Professional Recruiters/Real Estate Agents

Please contact us for a Commercial membership. Only qualified accounts with a product that caters to expats will be considered.

Registered Non-profits (no side hustle fake charities/orgs)

All NGO's/Non-profits posting must meet the following requirements

  • Non-profit must be registered in the EU/Poland
  • Non-profit must provide a publicly sharable ID number that is unique to the NGO in the post.
  • Admins will remove any posts that seek to divide our community, or are overly one-sided.

No exceptions

Failure to abide by these rules will result in immediate ban from this community per discretion of the admins.
